Navigating the Waters

An Indigenous public art walk celebrating the enduring presence of Coast Salish peoples on this land.

Stop 1 — The Ripple • Stop 2 — Salmon Project

"Coquitlam" derives from the kʷikʷəƛ̓əm word meaning "red fish up the river" — referring to the sockeye salmon that once flourished in local waters. This walk guides you through two public artworks that narrate the profound connection between Indigenous peoples, local ecosystems, and community.
